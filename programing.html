Comments

Put two slashes in front of a line of javascript to make it a comment. That means those lines will not be executed

Variables

In order to remember something and come back to it later, store it in a variable. You do this by typing var name ="Naeem". You can refer to it afterwards by just typing name.

Types of variables

  • String - characters written inside qoutes
  • Integer - helps you work with numbers
  • Long - Extra large or extra small numbers can be written with scientific (exponent) notation
  • Booleon - only uses ones and zeros
